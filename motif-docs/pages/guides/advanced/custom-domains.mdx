You can add a custom domain to your service without touching AWS.

1. In your `flightcontrol.json` file, add the `domain` config to your fargate service
    - See the [domain config docs](/guides/config)
2. Commit and push your `flightcontrol.json` changes
3. Once deployed, the required DNS records will be visible in the Flightcontrol dashboard.
