## For Anyone: $5,000 Credits for 2 years

### Option 1

Get $5,000 free credits by signing up for [YC Startup School](https://www.startupschool.org/), a free online course.

### Option 2

Get $5,000 free credits by paying $149 to [https://www.joinsecret.com/offers/aws-credits-startups](https://www.joinsecret.com/offers/aws-credits-startups).

## If Bootstrapped

Get $1,000 free credits by applying to [AWS Activate](https://aws.amazon.com/activate).

## If VC funded/part of accelerator

Get $100,000 free credits by applying to [AWS Activate](https://aws.amazon.com/activate).
